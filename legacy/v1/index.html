04 · Break it

What happens when the check never passes?

Break the verifier so it always fails. Then decide whether the safety stop is watching your back.

The takeaway

A verifier that never passes means the success stop never fires. Without a safety stop, the loop just… keeps spending.

Every loop needs two exits:
one for success, one for safety.

07 · Where next

Three things to carry with you

The four beats

Reason → Act → Observe → Check, on repeat. That cycle is the whole engine of an agent.

✓⛔
Two ways to stop

A success stop when the verifier passes, and a safety stop when steps run out. Never ship a loop with only one.

🎯
The verifier steers

Your check is the steering wheel. A sharp, testable verifier is what turns a chatbot into a system that gets things right.

You can now explain loop engineering to someone else. That's the whole point.
